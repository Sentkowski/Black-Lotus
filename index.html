Who we are

We're all from the same high school. So it's no big surprise that this is where Black Lotus began. It was around 2010.

Back then we were busy switching instruments, exploring different music genres and, of course, studying for exams. We hardly even talked to each other.

Only at the end of high school we decided to start a music project together. Later, as we realised our group's potential, it transformed into a band.

From then on we spent countless hours not only practising, but also listening to each other. There were less and less dissonances between us as we started to form a perfect harmony.

And now, this harmony is presented to everyone in the form of Bloom. It consists of years of dialogue, friendship and open-mindedness. Try it.
